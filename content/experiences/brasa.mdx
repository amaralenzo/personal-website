[BRASA](https://gobrasa.org), the Brazilian Student Association, was my home after leaving Brazil.

I went to Purdue not knowing a single soul, scared, but very eager to find my place, and BRASA helped me do exactly that.

I started by being a member in the local Purdue chapter, which then turned into becoming a software engineering analyst for the global chapter, which finally culminated into me being the president of the Purdue chapter, taking care of more than 250 Brazilian students on campus, all while I still helped build the BRASA website, systems for our yearly conferences, and much more.

In my heart, I will forever be at [BRASA](https://gobrasa.org).
