I spent my freshman and sophomore summers at
[BTG Pactual](https://btgpactual.com), the largest investment bank in Latin
America, working on payment systems and applied AI.

BTG was my first real workplace, where I learned how software is actually
built when there are **a lot** of customers involved. In my first internship,
I worked on the Payment Processing team (**$2B a month!**), where I
optimized SQL queries, rewrote APIs, and shipped new features around
airport lounge access.

In my second internship, I joined a new team focused on
building AI tools to automate processes and make BTG employees more
productive. I led a team of two other interns to build an LLM-powered
pipeline that generated and analyzed investment fund contracts and related
documents. I still stay in touch with the team, and I'm happy to know it's
*still* being used!
