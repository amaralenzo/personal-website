I hated that there were no free and **good** campaign managers I could use to
collaborate with my group when running my D&D campaign, so I built
[Tavernkeep](https://tavernkeep.app). I co-founded it with my friend Rafa,
and I'm the sole engineer behind it.

It gives a table everything it needs to run and manage a campaign in one
place: collaborative wikis, interactive maps, live character sheets, and
real-time multiplayer editing.

Tavernkeep is **completely free** and sits at ~100 monthly active users, all
from a single Reddit post and word of mouth. This is my passion
project, and I don't think I'll ever stop building it.
