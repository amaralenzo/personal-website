I was one of three engineers at [Astor](https://astor.app) (YC S25),
building an AI investment advisor that lives in your phone.

At Astor, I fell in love with startups. I didn't know it was possible to
learn that much in four months, but Astor kept proving me wrong. In those
four months we built the product, launched it, and grew to **5,000+ users**
and **$250M+ in connected assets**.

I worked deeply across the whole stack: the iOS app, the agentic advisor, evals, context management, and a lot more.
In the last month
and a half, I was asked to also be a part of the Growth team, where I automated our
marketing, built our
[Growth AI Employee](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/bruno-koba_our-intern-coded-an-agent-version-of-himself-ugcPost-7460397189500977152-8atB/) end-to-end,
and had a lot of fun writing r/wallstreetbets posts (1+ million views).
