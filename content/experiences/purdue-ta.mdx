In fall 2025 I was a teaching assistant for CS 250 (Computer Architecture)
at [Purdue](https://purdue.edu). I've always loved teaching and helping
people, so this was an easy fit.

I supported **~200 students** through weekly labs on systems fundamentals,
from breadboards, to memory architecture, to assembly, guiding them
through hands-on exercises in C.

I met a lot of cool students and made friends along the way, and it paid
for my weekly Raising Cane's.
